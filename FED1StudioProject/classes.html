Class 1. The beginner class. Have you thought of growing the beard? Have you grown one and were then forced to get rid of it by small children, loved ones, and/or your employer? Start here.
Class 2. The not quite a beginner. So you've started growing the beard. Congratulations that's the first step. Now it just takes commitment. Keep it up. We're here for you.
Class 3. Day 3, it's Wednesday. Other might be noticing the beard. Co-workers might start to ask if everything is OK at home. It's ok to tell them with confidence that the beard is coming.
Class 4. Day 4, By now you might be wondering if you're meant to have the beard. And maybe the spiritual beard is your path.
Class 5. Here we start to discuss and recognize that the beard is nothing but a state of mind. Whether you have a burley beard or an go months and no one notices, the beard is a state of mind.
Class 6. Just relax. It's Saturday. Whether you just started Monday of this is your 500th day of growth, we all recognize that the beard is nothing but a state of mind. You might have a spiritual beard and that's ok. All beards are welcome the physical and metaphysical.
